HABITAT

Scribbled Angel Fish

The Scribbled Angelfish, named for its abundance of intricate markings, is a very popular angelfish species that is endemic to the Coral Sea along the Great Barrier Reef of northeastern Australia.

Scarlet Macaw

Scarlet macaws are native to the tropical rainforest areas of Central and South America. Its preferred habitat is humid evergreen forests at elevations from about 1,000 to 3,000 feet.

Poison Dart Frog

With a range of bright colors—yellows, oranges, reds, greens, blues—poison dart frogs aren't just big show-offs either. Those colorful designs tell potential predators, "I'm toxic. Don't eat me."
